About us

We are a group of 5 second year Computer Science students at the University of Bristol working to implement a "Virtual Microbiology Lab Simulation". Our client is Dr Isabel Murillo Cabeza, a lecturer in the School of Cellular and Molecular Medicine also at the University of Bristol and it is her idea we are implementing.

Overview

The project can be described as a virtual microbiology lab simulation. What we mean by this is that the system should simulate a laboratory environment in which microbiology students can complete experiments interactively. The system may also incorporate a game aspect, say scoring system, to increase enjoyment of learning.

By Leifan Lei, Ruhan Hou, Patrick Gao, Yue Wu and Joshua Hall
